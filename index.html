FluoGen: An Open-Source Generative Foundation Model for Fluorescence Microscopy Image Enhancement and Analysis

FluoGen-Group
University of science and technology of China, School of Engineering
2025
FluoGen Open Platform

We present FluoGen, a diffusion-based generative foundation model designed to improve the performance of AI-based methods in data-constrained fluorescence image processing.

Visual comparison of super-resolution results from FluoGen-s and other state-of-the-art methods on typical organelles (F-actin, microtubules, ER, and CCPs) from the BioSR dataset. Corresponding ground truth (GT) images are shown for reference.

Large-scale, heterogeneous fluorescence microscopy dataset for generative pre-training. The scale and diversity of the dataset provide a robust foundation for learning generalizable representations of cellular organization.
